About Us

Unity Grounds Coffee House is a celebration of our diverse roots and shared love for exceptional coffee.

Unity Grounds was born from the shared vision of three friends coming together in the vibrant, welcoming heart of London.

Our name is our promise: a place where flavours travel and people meet in the middle. We source directly and responsibly from smallholder partners in Thailand, Somalia, and India, paying fairly and building long-term relationships that make every cup traceable and worth talking about.

In the café, we keep it simple and thoughtful—small-batch roasts, seasonal menus, and pastries from local makers. We minimise waste, offer lower-impact milk options, and give our spent grounds to nearby gardens. It’s coffee with craft and care, shaped by our cultures and this incredible city.

— The Unity Grounds Team

  • Direct/relationship coffee from Thailand, Somalia, India
  • Local makers for pastries and provisions
  • Seasonal rotations & transparent sourcing
  • Lower-impact choices and waste-smart bar flow
